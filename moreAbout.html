Why is important learn Cybersecurity?

It’s important to learn about cybersecurity to keep our devices and information safe. This helps us stop bad people from stealing things like photos, passwords, or money.
If we know how to protect ourselves online, we can use the internet to play, learn, or chat without worries. By learning cybersecurity, we also help our family and friends stay safe from tricks or scams online.
